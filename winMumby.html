Win Mumby



What is it About


It is a basketball competition hosted by Woodstock School, inviting top schools from all over India of the sport. It is tournament where the top teams from each bracket compete in a knockout fashion to win the trophy. This tournament has been happening every year, for the pasat 25 yrs.

Requirements for Registration


Age Requirements: 14 yrs of age, or above
Conditions: Can only apply as a school, not individual players.
How to Register: Go to the webpage at the url sports.woodstock.ac.in. Go to the Win Mumby page through Tournaments on the menu bar, and scroll to the bottom. Download the forms attached, fill them out as a school (they will ask for school details, player details, and player preferences) and send the forms to Woodstock School via the email, principal@woodstock.ac.in.
Registration Link: http:://add-url-here.com

Venue


This basketball tournament takes place in the Win Mumby Gym on the Woodstock School campus. Woodstock School is located in Mussoorie, Uttarakhand, India.
